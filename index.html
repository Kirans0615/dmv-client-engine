FAQ

Fair questions.

Why would you design something for free?

Because it's the fastest way to prove I'm good, and most owners who see their draft want it live. If you don't, I've spent a couple of hours and you got a free design — that trade works for me.

What's the catch with the monthly plan?

There isn't one: the build cost is spread into the monthly rate so you don't pay a big invoice upfront. Cancel anytime — the domain is registered in your name, so it's always yours.

I already have a website. Can you fix it?

Yes — most of my work is replacing sites that don't work on phones, load slowly, or say "© 2019" in the footer. Same deal: I'll draft the redesign free first.

Do you do online ordering / booking / payments?

I connect the tools you already use (Square, Toast, Booksy, Calendly, etc.) so everything lives in one place. If you don't have one yet, I'll recommend the cheapest option that fits.

Are you actually local?

Yes — DMV based. For clients in DC, Arlington, Alexandria, and close-in Maryland I'll happily meet in person.
